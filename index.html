What Information Do We Collect?

We focus on collecting only the essential information to make your wallpaper experience amazing:

  • App Usage Data: We track how you interact with the app. This includes which stunning wallpapers you browse, which ones you choose to download or set, and how you navigate through the app. This helps us understand what you love, so we can bring you more of it!
  • Device Information: We collect basic details about your device, like its type (e.g., iPhone, Samsung Galaxy), operating system version (e.g., iOS 18, Android 15), and a unique identifier for your device. This isn't about *you*, but about making sure the app runs smoothly and looks fantastic on *your specific device*.
  • Permissions You Grant: To let you save your favorite wallpapers or set them as your background, we'll ask for specific permissions like "Storage" or "Photos/Media." **Rest assured, these permissions are solely for enabling these features.** We never snoop around your personal files or collect anything beyond what's necessary for the app to function beautifully.

How Do We Use This Information?

Every piece of data we collect has a purpose: to make your C56 Wallpaper experience better!

  • To **keep the C56 Wallpaper app running flawlessly** and deliver beautiful content to you.
  • To **constantly improve our app and services**, adding new features and fixing any glitches.
  • To **personalize your experience** by recommending wallpapers you'll truly love, based on your viewing habits.
  • To **diagnose and fix any technical issues** you might encounter, ensuring a smooth experience.
  • To **understand overall usage trends**, which helps us prioritize what new wallpapers and features to develop next.

Your Data Security is Our Priority

We take the security of your information seriously. We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, accidental loss, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We're committed to keeping your information safe and sound.

Sharing Data? Only When It Makes Sense, Anonymously.

We want to be crystal clear: **we will absolutely NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for advertising or any other commercial purposes.**

Sometimes, we work with trusted third-party service providers (like Google Analytics, for example) to help us understand how the app is being used. When we do this, we only share **aggregated, non-identifiable information**. This means the data is combined and anonymized, so it can't be traced back to you personally. It's simply to help us analyze overall app performance and usage patterns.
